Travel Influencers

Dale Philip

Dale Philip, a former IT/software professional, made an interesting transition to becoming a professional poker player after achieving considerable success in the field. However, his poker career took an unexpected turn when he faced reported behavioral issues during an event in Marbella, Spain, leading to his removal from Team PokerStars Online. Despite this setback, Philip didn't let it deter him from pursuing new ventures.

Currently, Dale Philip has found a new passion as a YouTube travel vlogger, embarking on exciting adventures across more than 50 countries. His travel vlogs showcase his exploration of diverse cultures, breathtaking landscapes, and unique experiences. One of his notable moments was captured in a TikTok video posted in June 2020, where he engaged in a spirited debate online while bargaining for a street snack in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India.

However, Philip's journey hasn't been without challenges and controversies. In January 2021, he faced legal consequences when he ventured into the Munnar forest in Kerala, India without obtaining proper permission. This incident resulted in legal action being taken against him, emphasizing the importance of adhering to local regulations and respecting natural reserves. Another incident occurred during his visit to Clifton Beach in Karachi, Pakistan in May 2022.

Harald Baldr

Harald Baldr, a renowned Norwegian travel vlogger, has gained popularity by documenting his global adventures on his YouTube channel. With an extensive travel history of over seventy countries, he continually pushes boundaries and explores new destinations each year. Baldr emphasizes cost-saving measures, such as cutting his own hair, avoiding extravagant parties, and prioritizing essentials over luxuries, in order to allocate more funds towards his travels.

After embarking on his first journey from Thailand in October 2001, Baldr severed ties with his home country, Oslo, Norway. Despite warnings as a school kid in the 1990s about the perceived dangers of Thailand, he purchased a two-way ticket in 2002, fulfilling his dream of discovering this captivating Asian nation. This experience served as a gateway to exploring twenty different countries and made Thailand a cherished highlight.

Baldr's videos showcase his ability to effortlessly connect with people from various backgrounds. His natural and relatable interactions with individuals he encounters during his travels form the focal point of his content, drawing viewers in. Furthermore, his philanthropic endeavors inspire others, as he generously donates his income to support locals in need.

Sheneller

Shenelle and her partner Shehaan have been immersed in the world of travel content creation since 2018. As a dynamic and hard-working duo, they have honed their skills in directing, filming, and producing captivating content for a wide range of platforms, from social media to prime-time TV commercials. Their goal is to deliver disruptive campaigns that drive engagement by combining cutting-edge equipment, top-notch production standards, and a knack for innovative ideas.

With their extensive travels across Asia, Shenelle and Shehaan leverage their experiences and constantly evolving expertise to produce breathtaking cinematic content for the brands they collaborate with. Their repertoire includes an impressive array of work, spanning both local and international brands.

Armed with the latest video and audio technology, the duo ensures their clients receive nothing short of 4K cinema-grade footage accompanied by localized audio. Despite their proficiency, they maintain a lean and adaptable team, capable of capturing any occasion or experience.

Travel with Chris

An enthralling YouTube channel that takes viewers on unforgettable journeys around the world, including the captivating island nation of Sri Lanka. Led by the charismatic and intrepid explorer, Chris, this channel is a gateway to the wonders of travel and discovery.

Through his lens, Chris paints a vivid picture of Sri Lanka, showcasing its rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes. From the ancient ruins of Sigiriya to the pristine beaches of Mirissa, Chris delves into the heart and soul of this tropical paradise. With his keen eye for detail, he captures the essence of Sri Lanka's bustling markets, traditional festivals, and mouthwatering cuisine, inviting viewers to immerse themselves in the local experience.

But "Travel with Chris" is not just about showcasing popular tourist spots. Chris goes beyond the beaten path, venturing into lesser-known corners of Sri Lanka, unearthing hidden gems and connecting with the locals. His genuine curiosity and open-minded approach create authentic and meaningful encounters, leaving a lasting impact on both Chris and his audience.

Davud Akhundzada

At the age of 18, his adventure began in the heart of Europe as he moved to Prague, Czech Republic for his studies. For nine years, Prague became his cherished home, where he completed his education and built a meaningful life. During his corporate career in the pharmaceutical industry, he worked for esteemed companies and experienced rapid growth, holding three different positions across two departments.

Despite being offered another opportunity within the corporate world, he made the life-altering decision to leave his well-paid and secure job in September 2021. The choice to follow his true passion for travel was one of the scariest he had ever faced. Financial, physical, and emotional uncertainties loomed, but he knew he couldn't settle for a life confined to 9-to-5 routines or limited vacations.

Today, he is living his dream, dedicating himself to content creation and sharing his adventures with millions of viewers across various platforms. Traveling with just a 15-kilogram backpack, he immerses himself in the countries that his passport allows. Being a content creator never feels like a job; it's a deeply fulfilling endeavor. He invests his energy into building a future that is truly his, liberated from the corporate machine and the monotony of trading time for money.

Lanka Planet Travels

Sri Lanka, often referred to as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is a captivating destination known for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. From pristine beaches and lush tea plantations to ancient temples and wildlife-filled national parks, Sri Lanka offers a diverse range of experiences for travelers. Immerse yourself in the vibrant local culture, indulge in mouthwatering cuisine, explore UNESCO World Heritage sites, or embark on thrilling wildlife safaris. Whether you seek relaxation, adventure, or cultural immersion, Sri Lanka is sure to leave a lasting impression on your heart and soul.
