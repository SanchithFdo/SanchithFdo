Travel Around The World With Apps





Tripadvisor

Tripadvisor is an app that helps you
plan, book, and do all the other things you
need to do when traveling.

Booking.com

Booking.com is an app that helps you book flights, hotels, apartments, rental cars in minutes.

Hopper

Hopper is an app that keeps track of flight ticket pricings and
finds you the best time to buy your ticket

Maps.Me

Maps.me is an app that gives you access to offline maps around 36 countries. This incredibly detailed app is up to date and fast and reliable.

Kayak

Kayak is an app that looks through hundreds of travel sites at once and find you exactly what you want. Cheap flights, rental cars, great hotels and any other thing that you are missing in your trip.

Airbnb

Airbnb is an app that finds the perfect place for you to enjoy your dream vacation. With more than filters, you can find the best place that matches your liking.

Lanka Planet Travels

Sri Lanka, often referred to as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is a captivating destination known for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. From pristine beaches and lush tea plantations to ancient temples and wildlife-filled national parks, Sri Lanka offers a diverse range of experiences for travelers. Immerse yourself in the vibrant local culture, indulge in mouthwatering cuisine, explore UNESCO World Heritage sites, or embark on thrilling wildlife safaris. Whether you seek relaxation, adventure, or cultural immersion, Sri Lanka is sure to leave a lasting impression on your heart and soul.
